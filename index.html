Carbon & Place provides free tools to allow communities, planners, and policy makers to understand their local carbon footprint.

Based on the latest research and the best available data, we explore how carbon footprints change between neighbouhoods and how the path to net-zero will vary between places.

Place-Based Carbon Calculator (legacy)

Launched in 2020 by the Centre for Research in Energy Demand Solutions (CREDS). The PBCC allows you to view how neighbouhood carbon footprints vary across England. This version of the PBCC is now being archived in preparation for the launch of a new verion (see below).
