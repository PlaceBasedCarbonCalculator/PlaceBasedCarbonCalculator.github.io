Carbon & Place

Every Place Matters

Carbon & Place provides free tools to allow communities, planners, and policy makers to understand their local carbon footprint.

Tools

Place-Based Carbon Calculator

Understand your neighbouhoods carbon footprint an how it compares to other places.

Transport and Accessability Explorer

Transport is the largest source of emissions in the UK

Retrofit Explorer

Britain has some of the coldest and leakiest buildings in Europe, what can we do to fix them?

Land Use and Planning Explorer

How we use land and what we put in different places matters, explore how land is used across Great Britain.
